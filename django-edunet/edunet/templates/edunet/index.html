Tree of Knowledge

The Tree of Knowledge consists of keywords extracted from a lecture. There are keywords extracted from the lecture as a whole and keywords extracted from each paragraph within the lecture transcript. The number of keywords can be chosen up to, but not exceeding ten.

View details »

Puzzle of Knowledge

The Puzzle of Knowledge allows for an interactive experience. The student must complete a puzzle with the pieces that are given. The pieces are keywords and the most important ones must be placed in the center. To complete the puzzle, the pieces must then be placed in an order that spirals out.
